The Problem

  • Indian cities have more cameras than officers can manually monitor
  • Violations are missed, delayed, or inconsistently fined
  • No clear link between evidence → plate → violation → challan
  • Pure automation risks wrongful fines; pure manual review doesn't scale

1.35M+ road deaths globally per year — enforcement must be faster and fairer.

Our Solution

TrafficGuard AI is an intelligent traffic enforcement assistant that turns camera photos into accountable challans.

📷 Capture

Traffic image from CCTV, upload, or live webcam

🔍 Identify

Reads plate, detects violation type

⚖️ Fine

Calculates challan under Motor Vehicles Act

👮 Review

Borderline cases go to an officer before action

How It Works

1. Upload Image
→
2. Read Plate
→
3. Detect Violation
→
4. Assign Fine (₹)
→
5. Route Decision
  • High confidence → Auto-process challan
  • Medium confidence → Human review queue
  • Low confidence → Discard (no wrongful fine)

Why We're Different

  • Plate-linked challans — not just detection, full enforcement workflow
  • Indian law integration — MV Act fines, not generic labels
  • Uncertainty-aware routing — avoids auto-fining weak evidence
  • End-to-end platform — not a model demo, a working command center
  • Officer feedback loop — approve/reject improves future decisions
